Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26/11/2025

Invictum Fitness respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and what rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Invictum Fitness, we collect and store a limited amount of personal data necessary to provide the app's core functionality.

This includes:

  • Email address
  • A securely hashed password
  • Workout data, exercises, templates, logs, and other fitness-related data linked to your account

This data is used solely to enable account authentication, data synchronization, and app functionality. We do not collect unnecessary personal information.

If you don't create an account and log in, we do not have any access to your information. Everything can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing it's cache.

2. Account and Cloud Storage

When you sign in, your data is stored securely on our servers so it can be accessed across devices and restored if needed.

Only data required for the operation of the app is stored. We do not access or analyze your personal workout data for any other purpose.

To have your account removed, please contact us at info@invictum-fitness.com.

3. Third-Party Services

Invictum Fitness does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics services. Data is only shared if required by law.

4. Calorie and Goal Calculations

The app includes features for calculating daily calorie needs, workout goals, and other fitness-related recommendations. These calculations are based on standard estimation formulas.

Disclaimer:

The calculations provided by Invictum Fitness are intended as a general guideline only. Results may vary per individual and are not guaranteed to be 100% accurate.

Invictum Fitness does not provide professional medical, dietary, or fitness advice. Always consult a qualified professional if you require personalized guidance.

5. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

  • Passwords are stored using secure hashing methods
  • Access to user data is restricted and protected
  • We do not store passwords in plain text

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and manage your data within the app
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correct inaccurate information

To request account deletion, you can contact us via email.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be posted within the app or made available alongside the app listing. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects when changes were most recently applied.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

Email: info@invictum-fitness.com
