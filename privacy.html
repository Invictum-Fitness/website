Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26/11/2025

Invictum Fitness respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and what rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Invictum Fitness does not collect, store, or process any personal data outside of your own device.

All information you enter in the app (such as workouts, exercises, templates, nutrition logs, goals, achievements, and weekly updates) is stored locally on your device only.

We have no access to your data, and no information is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

The app does not collect:

  • Name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • Account details
  • Location data
  • Health data beyond what you manually enter
  • Advertising IDs
  • Device or analytics data
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Cloud-synced information

2. Local Data Storage

All app data is stored in a local database on your device.

This data is accessible only to you, and cannot be viewed, accessed, or retrieved by us.

If you clear the app cache all locally stored data will be permanently deleted from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Invictum Fitness:

  • does not use analytics tools
  • does not display ads
  • does not use tracking services
  • does not share any data with third parties.

4. Calorie and Goal Calculations

The app includes features for calculating daily calorie needs, workout goals, and other fitness-related recommendations. These calculations are based on standard estimation formulas.

Disclaimer:

The calculations provided by Invictum Fitness are intended as a general guideline only. Results may vary per individual and are not guaranteed to be 100% accurate.

Invictum Fitness does not provide professional medical, dietary, or fitness advice. Always consult a qualified professional if you require personalized guidance.

5. Security

Since your data is stored only on your device, it is protected by your device’s own security features.

We recommend:

  • Using a secure device lock (PIN, password, or biometrics)
  • Keeping your device updated with the latest security patches

6. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store any data, all control lies with you.

You can:

  • Manage, edit, or delete your data directly within the app
  • Clear the app cache to permanently remove all locally stored data

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be posted within the app or made available alongside the app listing. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects when changes were most recently applied.
