About

The goal is to make an app that helps people get healthier and fitter while keeping everything easy to use and enjoyable.

The App

Invictum Fitness

This app started as a school project. The goal was simple: build something functional, clean, and genuinely useful.

While working on it, I realized there was much more potential. What began as an assignment slowly turned into a personal project and eventually into a real app.

Over time, features were refined, design choices became more intentional, and the focus shifted from “just building” to actually helping people stay consistent with training and nutrition.

Today, the app continues to evolve. Not as a finished product, but as something that grows through learning, feedback, and real use.

No social feed. No distractions. Just progress.

Myself

Lucas

I've always enjoyed training and staying active. Like many others, I used apps to track workouts and nutrition, but I kept running into the same problem.

Workouts were in one app. Nutrition in another. Progress spread across multiple places.

On top of that, many essential features were locked behind paywalls. Things that felt basic like tracking, insights, overviews often required a subscription.

I wanted something different. One app where workouts and nutrition come together in a single, clear overview. No unnecessary complexity, and no paid barriers for the things that truly matter.

This app is built around that idea: keep the essentials accessible, free, and easy to use so staying consistent doesn't depend on subscriptions or multiple tools.
